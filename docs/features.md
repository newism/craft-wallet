# Features

## Apple Wallet + Google Wallet

Works on every iPhone and Android phone. Members add their card with one tap. The card lives on their lock screen, ready to scan at the door, at the bar, or at the front desk.

Whether your members carry iPhones or Android devices, they get the same seamless experience. One plugin handles both platforms: you set it up once, and every member is covered.

![Apple Wallet membership card](/screenshots/apple-pass.png)
![Google Wallet membership card](/screenshots/google-pass.png)

## QR Code Membership

Each pass includes a QR code containing the member's unique ID. Staff scan it at entry points, the bar, the pro shop, or anywhere membership needs to be verified.

The QR code works with any standard QR scanner: no proprietary hardware, no expensive POS integration. If you already have a scanner at reception, it works today.

## Automatic Updates

When staff update a member's details in Craft CMS, their pass updates automatically on their phone. Name changes, membership tier upgrades, profile photo updates: all pushed in real time through Apple and Google's native push infrastructure.

No re-issuing cards. No "please check your email for your new card." No manual steps. The member's phone just updates silently in the background.

## Customisable Design

Brand your passes with your venue's colours, logos, and imagery. Each pass carries your identity, not a generic template with someone else's branding.

The plugin provides sensible defaults that work out of the box. Developers can fully customise pass content through Craft events: add custom fields, change layouts, adjust branding for different membership tiers, or pull in data from other parts of your Craft CMS site.

## Your Data, Your Control

Bring your own members. Generate your own passes. Member names, IDs, and membership data live in your Craft CMS database, not a third-party platform. You control the Apple Developer account and Google Wallet API credentials directly.

No vendor lock-in. No data-processing agreements with a middleman. No external service interpreting your member data. If you ever move away from the plugin, your credentials and your data go with you.

## One Control Panel

Staff already manage the website in Craft CMS. Now they manage membership passes there too. View pass status, manage registered devices, and monitor updates, all from the same control panel they already know.

No switching between systems. No sync issues. No duplicate data entry. When a staff member updates a user profile, the pass updates automatically. It is one workflow, not two.

## Built for Hospitality

Wallet Passes is specifically designed for venues that issue membership cards: RSL clubs, golf courses, hotels, gyms, surf clubs, co-working spaces, wine clubs, and any organisation with a member base.

Replace physical PVC cards and PDF QR codes buried in email inboxes that members can never find when they need them.

Your members already have the best card delivery system in the world in their pocket. This plugin puts your membership card in it.
